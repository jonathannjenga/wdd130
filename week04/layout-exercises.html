Our History

The Rapids was founded on the day you were born — by your mind. Starting with just a handful of brain cells and a passion to dream. Our dreams grew, and we added many valuable members to our dream team. While personnel have moved to different teams over the years, our spirit has always remained strong.

Today, The Rapids stands as a leading provider of at-home white water rafting experiences. Just lean back, close your eyes, and let our team take you down the river.
