Our History

Whitewater Rafting Adventures was founded in 1998 by a team of outdoor enthusiasts who wanted to share their love of rivers and adventure. Over the years, we have expanded our trips, guiding thousands of rafters through some of the most scenic waterways in the world.

Our mission is to offer safe, sustainable, and thrilling experiences while fostering a deep respect for nature and local communities.
